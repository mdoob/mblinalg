Item 1.

If a matrix has an entry \(a_{i,k}\not=0\text{,}\) then multiply the row \(R_i\) by \(\frac 1{a_{i,k}}\text{,}\) that is, carry out the elementary row operation \(R_i\gets \frac 1{a_{i,k}}R_i\text{.}\) In the resulting matrix, the \(i\)-\(k\) entry is \(\frac {a_{i,k}}{a_{i,k}}=1\text{.}\)
