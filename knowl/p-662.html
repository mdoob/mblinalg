Consider the system of linear equations

\begin{align*} x_1+x_2+x_3 \amp =2\\ x_1-x_2+x_3 \amp =0\\ 2x_1-x_2+x_3 \amp =2\text{.} \end{align*}

We have

\begin{align*} A \amp = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \amp 1\amp 1\\ 1 \amp -1 \amp 1 \\ 2 \amp -1 \amp 1 \end{bmatrix} \amp \det A \amp =2\\\\ A_1 \amp = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \amp 1\amp 1\\ 0 \amp -1 \amp 1 \\ 2 \amp -1 \amp 1 \end{bmatrix} \amp \det A_1 \amp =4 \\\\ A_2 \amp = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \amp 2\amp 1\\ 1 \amp 0 \amp 1 \\ 2 \amp 2 \amp 1 \end{bmatrix} \amp \det A_2 \amp =2\\\\ A_3 \amp = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \amp 1\amp 2\\ 1 \amp -1 \amp 0 \\ 2 \amp -1 \amp 2 \end{bmatrix} \amp \det A_3 \amp =-2 \end{align*}

and so \(x_1=\frac42 =2\text{,}\) \(x_2=\frac22 =1\text{,}\) and \(x_3=\frac{-2}2 =-1\text{.}\)
