Consider the system of four linear equations in four unknowns:

\begin{gather*} a_{1,1}x_1+a_{1,2}x_2+a_{1,3}x_3+a_{1,4}x_4=b_1\\ a_{2,1}x_1+a_{2,2}x_2+a_{2,3}x_3+a_{2,4}x_4=b_2\\ a_{3,1}x_1+a_{2,2}x_2+a_{3,3}x_3+a_{3,4}x_4=b_3\\ a_{4,1}x_1+a_{4,2}x_2+a_{4,3}x_3+a_{4,4}x_4=b_4 \end{gather*}

In each case, suppose that the given matrix is the reduced row echelon form of the augmented matrix. Find all of the solutions. to the original system of linear equations.
