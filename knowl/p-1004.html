  • \(L_1\) has equation \((x,y,z)=\vec{X_1}+t_1\vec{n_1}\text{,}\) \(-\infty \lt t \lt\infty\)

  • \(L_2\) has equation \((x,y,z)=\vec{X_2}+t_2\vec{n_2}\) \(-\infty \lt t \lt\infty\)
