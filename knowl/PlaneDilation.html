Example 6.1.7. A dilation in the plane.

We start with a real number \(s\gt 0\text{.}\) For a point \(\vec x\text{,}\) let \(L(\vec x)=s\vec x\text{.}\) If \(\vec x\not=\vec0\text{,}\) then \(L(\vec x)\) is on joining \(\vec x\) and \(\vec 0\text{.}\) The distance from \(\vec x\) to \(\vec 0\) has been stretched by a factor of \(s\) to get the distance from \(L(\vec x)\) to \(\vec 0\text{.}\)
