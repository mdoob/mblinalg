Theorem 4.5.6. Additional cross product properties.

Let \(\vec u\) and \(\vec v\) be vectors in \(\mathbb{R}^n\text{,}\) and let \(r\) be a real number. Then

  • \(\displaystyle \vec u\times\vec v=-(\vec v\times\vec u)\)
  • \(\displaystyle (r\vec u)\times\vec v=r(\vec u\times\vec v)\)
  • \(\displaystyle \vec u \times (\vec v+\vec w) =(\vec u \times \vec v)+(\vec u\times\vec w)\)
