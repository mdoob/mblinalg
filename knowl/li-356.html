Item 3.
Let \(D=P^{-1}AP\text{.}\) By direct computation we have
\begin{equation*} D=P^{-1}AP=\frac12 \begin{bmatrix} 1\amp 1\\ 1\amp -1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1 \amp 2 \\ 2 \amp 1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1\amp 1\\ 1\amp -1 \end{bmatrix} =\begin{bmatrix} 3\amp 0\\ 0\amp -1 \end{bmatrix}\text{.} \end{equation*}
