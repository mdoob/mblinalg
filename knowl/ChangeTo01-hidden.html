1. If a matrix has an entry \(a_{i,k}\not=0\text{,}\) then, with one elementary row operation, it may be changed to \(1\text{.}\)

  2. If some column \(k\) of a matrix has two non-zero entries \(a_{i,k}\) and \(a_{j,k}\) then, with one elementary row operation, (either) one of them may be changed to \(0\text{.}\)
