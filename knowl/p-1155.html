With no loss of generality, we may assume that \(\alpha\) is the largest angle. It may be acute or obtuse, but \(\beta\) and \(\gamma\) are definitely acute. That implies that we may drop a perpendicular from \(A\) that will divide the angle \(\alpha\) into \(\alpha_1\) and \(\alpha_2\) the side opposite into segements with lengths \(a_1\) and \(a_2\text{.}\)
