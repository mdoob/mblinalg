Theorem 2.9.1. Laws of Exponents.

For any invertible square matrix \(A\) and integers \(m\) and \(n\text{,}\)

  • \(\displaystyle A^mA^n=A^{m+n}\)

  • \(\displaystyle (A^m)^n=A^{mn}\)

  • \(\displaystyle (A^{-1})^{-1}=A\)

  • (\(A^n)^{-1}=(A^{-1})^n\)

  • \(\displaystyle (rA)^{-1}=\tfrac 1r A^{-1}\)
